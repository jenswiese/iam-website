First Release on May the 9th

May 5, 2021 | 1 minute read
Share this: Twitter Facebook Pinterest

Tags: release, collaboration, song

Okay, let’s get started. I’ll release the first song on May the 9th. The song is a collaboration between the talented Nadine de Macedo, songwriter, producer and remixer of pop, rock and electronic music based in Germany, and me.

It all started in February this year during FAWM where I left an instrumental called Sleepless without knowing what to do with it. And then, Nadine came up with lyrics, and helped me tweaking the arrangement to make it a short and catchy song.

And you can listen to Sleepless on May the 9th. Stay tuned, I’ll keep you posted!

By the way, you can listen to Nadines music on Soundcloud, and follow her on Facebook.
