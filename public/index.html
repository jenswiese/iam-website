The Project

My name is Jens! I’m a musician, producer and step sequencer enthusiast.

I AM MEADOWS is my approach to bring my favorite genres together – with almost no restrictions. Meaning: Synth meets guitar meets 808 meets jaw harp meets whatever!

Let’s see how this goes. :)

Currently you can listen to my music on:

  • Bandcamp,
  • Soundcloud and of course on
  • FAWM (if available),

First Release on May the 9th

Okay, let’s get started. I’ll release the first song on May the 9th. The song is a collaboration between the talented Nadine de Macedo, songwriter, producer and remixer of pop, rock and electronic music based in Germany, and me. It all started in February this year during FAWM where I left an instrumental called Sleepless without knowing what to do with it.
